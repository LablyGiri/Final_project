Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix all the ingredients well to form a dough.
  2. Shape into small balls.
  3. Fry until golden brown.
  4. Soak in sugar syrup before serving.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan.
  2. Add milk powder and sugar.
  3. Cook until thick.
  4. Spread in a tray, cool, and cut into pieces.

Peda

Ingredients

  • Khoya (Mawa)
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Pistachios (for garnish)

Recipe

  1. Heat khoya in a pan until soft.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder, mix well.
  3. Cook until mixture thickens.
  4. Shape into small discs and garnish with pistachios.

Kaju Barfi

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Boil sugar and water to make syrup.
  3. Add cashew powder and cook until mixture thickens.
  4. Spread on a greased plate, cool, and cut into diamond shapes.
